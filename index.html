Frequently Asked Questions

1. The bot is offline?

Although downtime is very minimal, the bot sometimes needs to go offline for updates or due to outages.

2. Why did my music stop!!

The bot & lavalink servers restart every day to help keep your music running smoothly. If your music stops during a restart, just play it again.
